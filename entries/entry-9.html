    SETPLAY

    Setplay Setplay fighters heavily rely on calculated setups. They shine in setplay situations - creating scenarios in which opponent has limited options where they can then easily capitalize. That usually striking the opponent whilst they are getting up after a knockdown and creating mix-up situations. Set play characters can overtake and pressure their opponents very easily by executing these pre-planned situations. However, they have very limited tools outside of this game plan if their opponent does not allow themselves to be set up. Toolkit: Strengths Unpredictable Strong knockdown power Weaknesses Limited tools outside setups How should I play as this fighter? How can I beat this fighter?
