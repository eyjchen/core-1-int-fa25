GRAPPLER

    Each character on the roster would have a regular grab, but grapplers have special ones called command grabs. Command grabs are different from regular grabs, usually dealing more damage and executed by a specific input (instead of just one or two buttons like a regular grab). Grapplers want to close the distance between the opponent and use these unique throw attacks. Once in range, these attacks deal a massive amount of damage. However, many of these moves have a slow start and tell tale signs that opponents can pick up on and react, so they require quite a bit of set up. They can also be comboâ€™d into or out of.
